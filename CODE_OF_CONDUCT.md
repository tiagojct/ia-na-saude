# Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) verbatim.

## TL;DR

- Be respectful. We disagree about pedagogy and AI in healthcare. Disagree on substance, not on people.
- No harassment, doxxing, slurs, sexual language directed at individuals, or sustained disruption.
- No clinical advice in issues or PR threads. This is a learning project; real patients go through real clinicians.
- Healthcare data (real PHI) in issues/PRs is grounds for immediate removal and ban.

## Reporting

Send to <tiago@tiagojct.eu>. Reports stay confidential.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, and any communication tagged with the project name.
